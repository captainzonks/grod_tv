First public release.

* Compose for TV UI: first-run, home (with D-pad-UP queue overlay), settings.
* Media3 ExoPlayer with MergingMediaSource for 1080p Piped streams.
* Persistent Room-backed queue with auto-advance.
* LAN HTTP API on port 7878, wire-compatible with the grod Rust daemon.
* mDNS service advertisement (_grod._tcp.local., device=grod-tv).
* Settings: Piped URL, quality, PIN, connectivity test.
