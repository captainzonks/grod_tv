D-pad focus fixes and small wire-protocol cleanup.

* HomeScreen: Settings button is now reachable from the idle state
  (D-pad CENTER opens settings instead of falling through to the
  PlayerView controls). PlayerView no longer steals D-pad input;
  remotes drive playback through grod_remote / the HTTP API.
* FirstRunScreen: OK button auto-focused on entry.
* SettingsScreen: Piped URL field auto-focused; Back navigates home.
* /status: PlaybackPhase.Error now surfaces as state="error" instead
  of being silently mapped to "idle".
* Release workflow: .sha256 sidecar references the final
  grod_tv-v<tag>.apk filename so `sha256sum -c` passes out of the
  box.
